About my site

We may ask why should we be Krishna conscious ?
What is the need ? We have all the opulence, we go shopping, we study in renowned universities, etc so why should we indulge in Krishna consciousness. Let us find out.

We all must have gone through with many emotions, sometimes we hurt others, and sometimes we get hurt. Sometimes we become happy and sometimes sad. Sometimes we have money and sometimes we don't.Something we win and sometimes we lose. This shows how temporary this material world is. These days depression, headaches are becoming so common, and there are family disputes going on. Somehow we all don't get satisfied, while performing our duties. Then we try to find something, our real identity, our real home, with a hope that after realising which we will become happy forever.
Srimad Bhagavatam, the jewel of Vedic literature, tells in text 4.4.44 that "Bhagavad Gita is the instruction given by Lord Krishna". Lord Krishna says in Bhagavad Gita text 9.17:
"पिताहमस्य जगतो माता धाता पितामह: । वेद्यं पवित्रम् ॐकार ऋक् साम यजुरेव च ॥ १७ ॥"
That is "I am the father of this universe, the mother, the support and the grandsire. I am the object of knowledge, the purifier and the syllable oṁ. I am also the Ṛg, the Sāma and the Yajur Vedas." Thus, we are the children of Lord Hari and this is our true identity. When we forget this, then we get entangled in several material miseries.
